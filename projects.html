What is this about?

VÉL403G is a manufacturing processes course tought by Hafliði Ásgeirsson at the University of Iceland during the spring semester of 2020. The course is a mandatory 6 ECTS endeavour for all mechanical engineering students and consists mostly of independent project work.

During these projects all performed work will be documented here on this website under the relevant link. The projects are ordered in chronologically descending order.
